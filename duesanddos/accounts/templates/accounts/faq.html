Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about managing your household with Dues & Do's.

Is Dues & Do's really free?

Yes! Our core features for tracking expenses and scheduling chores are completely free for households of up to 10 members.

How do I invite my roommates?

Once you create a household, you'll get a unique invite link. Just share that link with your roommates via text or email, and they can join instantly.

Does Dues & Do's handle actual payments?

Currently, we help you track balances and settle up records. We don't process direct bank transfers yet, but we provide easy links to note when a payment has been made via other apps.

What happens if someone forgets a chore?

The app sends gentle, automated reminders so you don't have to be the "bad guy." You can also see a history of chore completion to keep things fair.

How secure is my data?

We take privacy seriously. Your household data is private to your members, and we never sell your personal information to third parties.
